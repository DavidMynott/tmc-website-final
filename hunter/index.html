AHA Marksmanship Handbook

Before you attempt your shooting qualification, download and read this handbook. It covers the practical shooting principles that will help you meet the AHA standard. Take it with you to the range.

What's inside:

→ The Five Fundamentals — Aiming, Breath Control, Hold, Trigger, Follow Through

→ Firing Positions — Prone, Sitting, Kneeling, Standing

→ The B.R.A.S.S. Method — your shot sequence for every round

→ Field Marksmanship — zeroing, shot placement, wind, rests

→ Shotgun Techniques — swing-through and sustained lead

→ Your AHA Standard — 5 shots, 75mm, 100 metres
