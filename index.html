A note about the Australian Hunting Association

Every operator on The Marksmen Co holds a current AHA certificate. The Australian Hunting Association (AHA) is the national body that sets the professional standard for pest management operators in Australia — covering training, shooting proficiency, and professional conduct.

AHA certification is what makes The Marksmen Co different from an informal directory. When you see an operator profile on this platform, you can be confident they have completed a professional training program, demonstrated shooting proficiency, and committed to a published standard of conduct.

For farmers, this means every profile on the platform represents someone who has been assessed — not just someone who signed up.

How It Works

A simple, structured process connecting farmers with AHA-certified operators

1

Farmer posts a request

Farmers register their property and describe their pest management needs. Registration is free and takes less than 5 minutes. They specify the species, location, and timeframe.

↓
2

Operators are notified

AHA-certified operators within range of the property receive a notification on their jobs board. They can view the job details and express interest with one click. Their full profile is attached to their expression of interest.

↓
3

Farmer reviews and selects

The farmer sees which operators have expressed interest. They review each profile — AHA certification, species capability, calibres, insurance status, experience, and bio. They select the operator they want to work with.

↓
4

Direct connection via phone

On selection, the farmer and operator receive each other's contact details — phone number and email. From there, they coordinate directly via their own phones. The Marksmen Co is not involved beyond the introduction.

Important things to understand

TMC does not assign or coordinate

The Marksmen Co is a platform for introductions. We do not assign operators, supervise activity, or guarantee outcomes. Every decision belongs to the farmer.

Operators act independently

Every operator on the platform holds their own firearms licence and operates under their own legal responsibility. TMC does not grant firearms authority of any kind.

All operators are AHA certified

Every profile on The Marksmen Co represents someone who has completed the AHA Foundation training course and passed a shooting proficiency test. This is the baseline — not the exception.
